Overview

This privacy policy covers all apps published by Shabaka Malik Banks under the malikbanksnyc.com umbrella — currently MadWeather and MomKnows!. Both apps are designed with privacy as a core principle. Neither app sells, shares, or monetizes your personal data.

Both apps display "Data Not Collected" on their App Store privacy nutrition labels, with a single noted exception for MadWeather's use of location (described below).

What each app uses

MadWeather

MadWeather sends your device's location to Apple WeatherKit to retrieve weather forecasts. This location data is sent directly to Apple's servers and is not logged, stored, or retained by MadWeather or its developer. No other data leaves your device. MadWeather does not require an account and does not collect analytics, crash reports, or advertising identifiers.

MadWeather's App Store nutrition label reports "Data Not Collected" across all categories. Location is used transiently for weather lookups and is not linked to your identity.

MomKnows!

MomKnows! makes zero network calls — none at all, not even to check the time. All data you enter stays entirely on your device. Sensitive fields are stored in the iOS Keychain, encrypted by the operating system. The only way data leaves your device is if you explicitly choose to export an AES-256 encrypted local backup or a PDF — both of which remain in your control.

MomKnows! has no server, no account system, no analytics, and no crash reporting. Its App Store nutrition label reports "Data Not Collected" across all categories, with no exceptions.

No third-party SDKs

Neither MadWeather nor MomKnows! includes third-party SDKs of any kind — no analytics frameworks, no crash reporters, no ad networks, no tracking pixels, no social media integrations. Both apps are built entirely with first-party Apple frameworks.

Apple platform services

Both apps use standard Apple platform services:

  • Apple WeatherKit (MadWeather only) — for weather data retrieval
  • StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases through the App Store
  • iOS Keychain (MomKnows! only) — for secure on-device storage of sensitive fields

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. No data is shared with any non-Apple third party.

Children's privacy

Neither app is directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through one of our apps, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
