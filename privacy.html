Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the MadWeather mobile application ("App"), website (malikbanksnyc.com/madweather), and all related services (collectively, "Services"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

We collect your device's location to provide accurate weather forecasts for your area. Location data is processed locally on your device and through Apple WeatherKit. You can control location access through your device settings.

Authentication Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Phone number (for SMS authentication via Firebase)
  • Device identifiers for authentication purposes

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • App usage statistics and interactions
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Advertising interactions (via Google AdMob)

Email Address (Optional)

If you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect your email address through ConvertKit for product updates and announcements.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide accurate, location-based weather forecasts
  • Authenticate your account and maintain security
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements (free version)
  • Send product updates if you've subscribed to our mailing list
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Apple WeatherKit

Provides weather data. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Firebase (Google)

Handles authentication and backend services. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google AdMob

Displays advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

ConvertKit

Manages email subscriptions for our mailing list. Subject to ConvertKit's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Weather data and preferences are cached locally on your device. Authentication data is stored on Firebase's secure servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. In-App Purchases

We offer a one-time "ad-free forever" purchase through Apple's StoreKit 2. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information. Purchase records are maintained by Apple and linked to your Apple ID.

6. Your Privacy Rights

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if your information is sold or shared (we do not sell your data)
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (not applicable as we don't sell data)

To exercise these rights, contact us at malikbanksnyc@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide Services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email if you've subscribed to our mailing list. Continued use of Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your privacy rights:

Email: malikbanksnyc@gmail.com
