madweather

NYC weather. Real talk.

A weather app built for New Yorkers who keep it real. No fluff, no corporate meteorology speak — just the raw conditions, delivered with the same energy as the city itself. Borough-by-borough breakdowns, NYC slang forecasts, and achievements for surviving every season the city throws at you.

What it does

Real NYC Energy

Forecasts written in the language of the city. When it's 32° and snowing, you'll know it's "deadass OD brick." No sugarcoating.

Boro at a Glance

See conditions across all five boroughs simultaneously — Manhattan, Brooklyn, Bronx, Queens, and Staten Island, all in one view.

NYC Seasons & Achievements

Track NYC's real seasons — Fools Spring, Second Winter, Spring of Deception — and unlock borough achievements as you explore the city.
