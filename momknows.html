How It Works

Three pillars.
One complete picture.

MomKnows! works in both directions — capture knowledge from the people who raised you, and build your own record for the people who come after.

01

Ask

Guided Questions

200 curated questions across 5 categories, designed to surface the knowledge people forget to share — until it's too late. Work through them together, or send them ahead of a visit.

02

My Life Profile

Your Own Record

A secure personal record of your identity, medical history, insurance, emergency contacts, and vital information — organized so the people who love you can find what they need.

03

Export & Backup

Yours to Keep

Export a clean, readable PDF to share or print. Create an AES-256 encrypted local backup for safekeeping. Your data, fully in your hands — always.
